**Every Street** is a personal driving companion built around one simple idea: transforming your everyday driving into a clear, visual story of where you've been and what's left to explore.

It helps you track your real-world movement, see your street coverage progress, and plan smarter routes so that every drive has a purpose.

## What It Is

Every Street is a map-first experience that combines your trip history, coverage progress, and driving insights into a single, beautiful dashboard.

Instead of just logging endless miles, it shows you how those miles connect:
- **The Known:** Which streets you have already driven.
- **The Unknown:** Which streets are still untouched.
- **The Routine:** Where your habits are forming.
- **The Journey:** How your driving changes over time.

## What It's For

Every Street is designed for people who want to make their time on the road more meaningful. Use it to:

- **Complete Challenges:** Conquer street coverage goals in your neighborhood, city, or custom areas.
- **Drive with Purpose:** Plan routes that fill in missing streets instead of repeating the same loops.
- **Preserve Memories:** Keep a clean, searchable timeline to revisit your most meaningful drives.
- **Discover Patterns:** Understand your driving distance, frequency, destinations, and time spent on the road.
- **Track Your Life:** See the places, visits, and routines that naturally emerge from your driving.

## The Core Experience

We've built Every Street to feel like a living, breathing driving dashboard:

- **Coverage Map:** Watch your progress come to life as you conquer new roads.
- **Trip Journal:** Turn raw driving data into a readable, beautiful timeline.
- **Navigator:** Let the app guide you toward streets you haven't covered yet.
- **Insights:** Uncover personal trends, records, and movement patterns.
- **Places:** Highlight where you go the most and how your habits evolve.

## Who It's For

Every Street works perfectly for:

- **Goal Setters** who love measurable progress.
- **Explorers** determined to see every road in their area.
- **Optimizers** who enjoy turning daily commutes into long-term achievements.
- **Anyone** who wants their driving data to be visual, useful, and inspiring.
